## 1. Recitals and Purpose

This Independent Contractor Agreement (this "**Agreement**") is entered into by and between **The Candeed Corp**, a New Jersey corporation doing business as **Munchappy** ("**Munchappy**," "**Company**," "**we**," "**us**," or "**our**"), and you, the undersigned contractor ("**Contractor**," "**you**," or "**your**"), an independent contractor engaged in the business of performing delivery and related services as contemplated by this Agreement.

### 1.1 About the Platform

Munchappy operates an online marketplace and delivery facilitation platform, known to courier-facing participants as **Munchappy Courier** (the "**Platform**" or "**Courier Platform**"), that connects customers with independently operated restaurants and independent delivery contractors in areas where Munchappy operates. Munchappy's technology facilitates the transmission of customer delivery requests to Contractors, the coordination of pickup and delivery logistics, and the processing of payments on behalf of all parties.

Munchappy is not a restaurant, food preparer, food seller, or delivery company. Munchappy is a technology platform and marketplace facilitator.

### 1.2 About the Contractor

Contractor is an independently established business or individual service provider that provides delivery services to customers who have contracted for delivery through the Munchappy Platform. Contractor desires to access the Platform to receive and fulfill Delivery Opportunities offered to Contractor therethrough.

Contractor understands and expressly acknowledges that:

- Contractor is **not** an employee of Munchappy or of any restaurant or customer;
- Contractor is providing delivery services **on behalf of Contractor's own independent business**, not on behalf of Munchappy;
- Contractor is **free to accept or decline** any Delivery Opportunity offered through the Platform, subject to the terms of this Agreement;
- Contractor has the **sole right to control the manner** in which deliveries are physically performed, subject to applicable law and the outcome specifications of each delivery; and
- Nothing in this Agreement guarantees Contractor any particular volume of Delivery Opportunities or earnings for any period of time.

### 1.3 Effective Date

This Agreement becomes effective on the date Contractor accepts it, regardless of whether Contractor is at that time eligible to, or ever does, perform any Contracted Services.

## 2. Definitions

For purposes of this Agreement, the following terms shall have the meanings set forth below:

- **"Agreement"** means this Independent Contractor Agreement, together with all policies, guidelines, and supplemental terms incorporated herein by reference, as amended from time to time.

- **"Contracted Service"** means each individual Delivery Opportunity that Contractor accepts and agrees to complete.

- **"Contractor"** means the individual or business entity that has accepted this Agreement and is authorized to use the Courier Platform to receive and fulfill Delivery Opportunities.

- **"Courier Platform"** means the Munchappy Courier mobile application, related technology, and any associated tools or interfaces made available by Munchappy to Contractors for the purpose of receiving and fulfilling Delivery Opportunities.

- **"Customer"** means a person who places a food or beverage order through the Munchappy consumer platform and requests delivery of that order.

## 8. Daily Order Threshold and Session Termination

### 8.1 Daily Decline and Cancellation Threshold

Contractor may decline or cancel Delivery Opportunities up to a maximum of **three (3) times per calendar day** (the "**Daily Threshold**"), provided that in the case of a cancellation, the order has not yet been physically picked up from the Restaurant by Contractor at the time of cancellation.

Each of the following actions counts toward the Daily Threshold:

- Declining a Delivery Opportunity transmitted to Contractor through the Platform; or
- Cancelling a Contracted Service after acceptance but before physical pickup of the order from the Restaurant.

### 8.2 Session Termination Upon Reaching Threshold

Upon Contractor reaching the Daily Threshold of three (3) declines or cancellations in a single calendar day, **Contractor's active Session will be immediately and automatically terminated** by the Platform. Contractor will be unable to go online or receive any additional Delivery Opportunities for the remainder of that calendar day.

### 8.3 Daily Reset

The Daily Threshold resets at **midnight (12:00 AM Eastern Time)** each calendar day. Contractor may resume going online and receiving Delivery Opportunities beginning at midnight of the following calendar day.

### 8.4 In-Progress Deliveries

If Contractor has a Contracted Service in progress at the time the Daily Threshold is reached, Contractor remains obligated to complete that delivery in accordance with the terms of this Agreement. Session termination under this Section does not relieve Contractor of the obligation to complete any Contracted Service already accepted and in progress.

### 8.5 Repeated Threshold Violations

Munchappy reserves the right to review patterns of repeated Daily Threshold violations. Consistent or habitual reaching of the Daily Threshold may be reviewed under Munchappy's Deactivation Policy and may, at Munchappy's sole discretion, result in account suspension or permanent deactivation if Munchappy determines such conduct is inconsistent with Contractor's obligations under this Agreement.

## 10. Compensation and Payment Structure

### 10.1 Compensation Model — Customer-Funded

Contractor's compensation for each Contracted Service is **funded entirely and exclusively by the Customer** who has contracted for delivery services through the Munchappy Platform. Munchappy sets and collects the Gross Delivery Fee from the Customer on behalf of the Contractor as part of the order transaction, and remits the Net Earnings to Contractor following completion of the Contracted Service.

**Munchappy does not subsidize, supplement, or contribute to Contractor's compensation from its own funds.** The entirety of Contractor's earnings for each delivery originates from and is paid by the Customer as compensation for the delivery service contracted by the Customer. Munchappy's role is solely that of a marketplace facilitator and payment collection agent acting on behalf of Contractor in collecting the delivery fee from the Customer and remitting the appropriate net amount to Contractor.

### 10.2 Gross Delivery Fee

The Gross Delivery Fee for each Delivery Opportunity is determined by Munchappy at the time the Delivery Opportunity is generated, based on factors including but not limited to delivery distance, area of service, and demand conditions. The Gross Delivery Fee is presented to the Customer at the time of order placement and constitutes the total amount charged to the Customer for the delivery service.

### 10.3 Munchappy Platform Fee

Upon completion of each Contracted Service, Munchappy deducts a **platform service fee** (the "**Munchappy Fee**") from the Gross Delivery Fee collected from the Customer. The Munchappy Fee is currently:

> **Six percent (6%) of the Gross Delivery Fee, plus $0.30 per completed delivery.**

Munchappy reserves the right to modify the Munchappy Fee with advance written notice to Contractor as provided in Section 23.

### 10.4 Net Earnings Guarantee

Munchappy guarantees that Contractor's **Net Earnings for each completed Contracted Service shall not be less than $12.00**, regardless of the applicable Munchappy Fee. To the extent that the Gross Delivery Fee minus the Munchappy Fee would result in a net amount below $12.00, Munchappy will adjust the Gross Delivery Fee charged to the Customer accordingly to ensure that Contractor's Net Earnings are no less than $12.00 per completed delivery. **Munchappy does not absorb the cost of this guarantee from its own funds — the Gross Delivery Fee is set at a level sufficient to satisfy the guarantee in all cases.**

Net Earnings for deliveries involving longer distances will be set above the $12.00 minimum in accordance with Munchappy's then-current distance-based rate schedule, as disclosed to Contractor at the time of each Delivery Opportunity.

### 10.5 Payout Disclosure at Offer

The full Net Earnings amount payable to Contractor for each Delivery Opportunity shall be disclosed to Contractor before Contractor accepts the Delivery Opportunity. By accepting a Delivery Opportunity, Contractor agrees to perform the Contracted Service in exchange for the disclosed Net Earnings amount.

### 10.6 No Tips

The Munchappy Platform does not facilitate tipping. The Gross Delivery Fee charged to the Customer represents the complete and final payment for the delivery service contracted. No additional gratuity is expected or facilitated through the Platform. If a Customer voluntarily offers a cash gratuity to Contractor outside of the Platform, such gratuity is a matter between the Contractor and the Customer, and Munchappy has no involvement in, knowledge of, or responsibility for any such transaction. Contractor is solely responsible for reporting any cash gratuities to the appropriate taxing authorities.

### 10.7 Payment Schedule — Weekly Payout

Contractor's Net Earnings for all Contracted Services completed in a given week will be transferred to Contractor via direct deposit on a **weekly basis**, in accordance with Munchappy's then-current payout schedule. Munchappy will notify Contractor of the applicable payout day and any changes thereto.

### 10.8 Instant Cashout

Munchappy may offer Contractor the option to receive earned Net Earnings prior to the scheduled weekly payout through an **instant cashout** feature, subject to availability. Munchappy reserves the right to charge a fee for each instant cashout transaction. The applicable fee, if any, will be disclosed to Contractor at the time of each instant cashout request. By electing to use the instant cashout feature, Contractor agrees to the applicable fee and all conditions associated with that feature.

### 10.9 Payment Processing

Payments to Contractor are processed through Munchappy's designated third-party payment processor (currently **Stripe**). Contractor is responsible for providing accurate and complete bank account or payment information and for keeping such information up to date. Munchappy is not responsible for lost or delayed payments resulting from incorrect or outdated payment information provided by Contractor.

### 10.10 Service Failure and Forfeiture

In the event of a Service Failure attributable to Contractor's action or omission, Contractor shall forfeit all or a portion of the Net Earnings for the affected Contracted Service, as determined by Munchappy in its reasonable discretion. See Section 11 for the payment dispute process.

### 10.11 Withholding for Fraud or Abuse

Munchappy reserves the right to withhold, adjust, or recoup all or a portion of any payment owed to Contractor if Munchappy reasonably believes that Contractor has engaged in fraud, manipulation of the Platform, or abuse of the payment system, including but not limited to GPS spoofing, falsification of delivery records, or exploitation of the compensation model. Any such withholding shall be subject to the dispute process in Section 11.

## 12. Photo Proof and PIN Enforcement

### 12.1 Pickup Photo Requirement

For each Contracted Service, Contractor may be required to submit a **photograph at the time of pickup** from the Restaurant, confirming that the order has been collected. This requirement is enforced through the Courier Platform as a condition of proceeding with the delivery.

This photograph serves as proof of delivery and is a mandatory condition of completing the Contracted Service and receiving payment. ### 12.3 PIN Enforcement

Where the Munchappy Platform requires Contractor to obtain and verify a **PIN code from the Customer** as a condition of completing a delivery handoff, Contractor agrees to comply with such requirement and not to mark an order as delivered without completing the required PIN verification. Circumventing or falsifying PIN verification is a material breach of this Agreement.

### 12.4 Purpose and Consent

Contractor acknowledges that the photo proof and PIN enforcement requirements exist to protect the interests of Customers and to ensure the integrity of the delivery process. By using the Courier Platform, Contractor consents to these requirements and acknowledges that failure to comply with them constitutes a breach of Contractor's obligations under this Agreement.
