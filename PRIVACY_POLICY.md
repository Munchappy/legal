## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how **The Candeed Corp**, a New Jersey corporation doing business as **Munchappy** ("**Munchappy**," "**we**," "**us**," or "**our**"), collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information in connection with the Munchappy mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "**Platform**").

By accessing or using the Platform in any capacity, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the data practices described herein. If you do not agree with this Policy, you must discontinue use of the Platform.

This Policy is incorporated by reference into the Munchappy Customer Terms of Service and the Munchappy Courier Independent Contractor Agreement.

---

## 2. Who This Policy Applies To

This Policy applies to all individuals who interact with the Platform in any of the following capacities:

- **Customers** — individuals who use the Munchappy consumer application to browse menus, place orders, and receive deliveries;
- **Couriers** — independent contractors who use the Munchappy Courier application to receive and fulfill delivery opportunities; and
- **Merchants** — restaurants and food service establishments that participate on the Platform to receive and fulfill customer orders.

Where data practices differ meaningfully between these groups, this Policy identifies those differences clearly. Where no distinction is made, the relevant provision applies to all users.

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Customers

#### 3.1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account or use the Platform as a customer, we collect:

- Full name, email address, and phone number;
- Account password (stored in encrypted form);
- Delivery addresses and saved locations;
- Payment and billing information, processed securely through Stripe — Munchappy does not store full payment card numbers on its own servers; and
- Order history, preferences, special delivery instructions, and communications with Munchappy support.

#### 3.1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect the following information when you use the Platform:

- Device information, including device model, operating system version, and application version;
- IP address and general geolocation derived from your network connection;
- Usage data, including screens viewed, features used, and interactions within the application; and
- Crash logs and performance data used to monitor and improve Platform stability.

#### 3.1.3 Location Information

We collect your location data to determine whether delivery service is available in your area, to identify nearby restaurants, and to provide accurate delivery coordination. Location is collected when you grant the application permission to do so. You may adjust location permissions through your device settings at any time, though doing so may limit the functionality of the Platform.

---

### 3.2 Couriers

#### 3.2.1 Information You Provide

When you apply to become a Munchappy Courier and during your use of the Courier Platform, we collect:

- Full legal name, email address, and phone number;
- Account password (stored in encrypted form);
- Government-issued photo identification, submitted during identity verification;
- Profile photograph;
- Bank account or payment information for payout purposes, processed through Stripe; and
- Communications with Munchappy support.

#### 3.2.2 Identity Verification and Biometric Data

To maintain platform integrity and protect the safety of customers, Munchappy requires all Couriers to complete identity verification through our designated third-party identity verification provider, **Didit**.

This process involves:

- Submission of a government-issued photo ID; and
- Capture of a liveness video or selfie, from which facial geometry data is extracted to confirm that the person submitting the application matches the identification provided.

**Munchappy does not receive, store, or process raw biometric templates, facial geometry data, or liveness videos on its own servers.** This sensitive data is processed and retained exclusively within the secure, encrypted systems of Didit. Munchappy receives only the verification result and the identity details necessary to complete onboarding, such as your legal name and date of birth as they appear on your identification.

By completing the identity verification process, you consent to the collection and processing of facial geometry data by Didit for the purpose of verifying your identity. Didit's own privacy practices govern the handling of biometric data and are available through Didit's platform.

#### 3.2.3 Background Check Information

Munchappy requires all Couriers to undergo a background check administered by a designated third-party provider. Information collected in connection with the background check is handled by that provider in accordance with their own privacy practices and applicable law, including the Fair Credit Reporting Act. Munchappy receives only the results of the background check and does not store the underlying records.

#### 3.2.4 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect the following information during your use of the Courier Platform:

- Device information, including device model, operating system version, and application version;
- IP address;
- Usage data and crash logs; and
- **Real-time location data** during active delivery sessions, transmitted continuously to Munchappy's systems via Firebase Realtime Database for the purposes of delivery coordination and customer-facing delivery tracking. Location tracking is active from the time you go online on the Courier Platform until you go offline or complete your active session.

#### 3.2.5 Delivery Records

We collect and retain records of each Contracted Service performed, including pickup and dropoff timestamps, delivery photographs submitted through the Platform, PIN verification records, and earnings data.

---

### 3.3 Merchants

#### 3.3.1 Information You Provide

When a restaurant onboards to the Platform and during ongoing use, we collect:

- Business name, address, and contact information;
- Authorized representative name, email address, and phone number;
- Menu information, including item names, descriptions, pricing, and photographs, as provided by the merchant;
- Bank account information for payout purposes, processed through Stripe; and
- Communications with Munchappy merchant support.

#### 3.3.2 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect transactional data associated with orders received through the Platform, including order details, timestamps, payout records, and refund activity.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

### 4.1 All Users

We use information collected from all users to:

- Operate, maintain, and improve the Platform;
- Authenticate your identity and maintain account security;
- Process transactions and facilitate payments;
- Provide customer support and resolve disputes;
- Comply with applicable legal obligations; and
- Protect the safety, security, and integrity of the Platform and its users.

### 4.2 Customers

In addition to the above, we use customer information to:

- Process and fulfill your orders and transmit them to the applicable restaurant;
- Coordinate delivery of your order through the dispatch of an available Courier;
- Display your delivery status and real-time Courier location during active deliveries;
- Apply and remit applicable sales tax on your orders as required under New Jersey's marketplace facilitator law; and
- Communicate with you regarding your orders, account, and Platform updates.

### 4.3 Couriers

In addition to the general uses above, we use Courier information to:

- Verify your identity and eligibility to participate on the Platform;
- Dispatch Delivery Opportunities to you based on your real-time location and availability;
- Track and display your location to customers during active deliveries;
- Calculate, process, and remit your Net Earnings via Stripe;
- Maintain delivery records including proof of delivery photographs and PIN verification; and
- Investigate reports of misconduct or Platform violations and, where warranted, cooperate with law enforcement authorities.

### 4.4 Merchants

In addition to the general uses above, we use merchant information to:

- Display your menu and facilitate customer orders;
- Transmit orders to your establishment in real time;
- Calculate and remit your menu revenue via Stripe, net of the applicable platform fee;
- Collect and remit applicable sales tax on platform orders on your behalf; and
- Communicate with you regarding orders, payouts, refund activity, and Platform updates.

### 4.5 What We Do Not Do

Munchappy does **not**:

- Sell your personal information to any third party;
- Use your personal information for targeted advertising or behavioral profiling; or
- Share your personal information with third parties for their own independent marketing purposes.

## 5. How We Share Your Information

### 5.1 With Other Platform Participants

To facilitate order fulfillment and delivery, Munchappy shares limited information between platform participants as follows:

- **Restaurants** receive the customer's first name, order details, special instructions, and delivery address, solely for the purpose of preparing and handing off the order.
- **Couriers** receive the customer's first name, last initial, delivery address, and drop-off instructions, solely for the purpose of completing the delivery.
- **Customers** receive the Courier's first name and real-time location during an active delivery for tracking purposes.

No party receives more information about another than is reasonably necessary to fulfill their role in the transaction.

### 5.2 With Third-Party Service Providers

We share information with third-party providers who assist us in operating the Platform. See Section 6 for a full list of providers and the data shared with each.

### 5.3 For Legal and Safety Purposes

We may disclose your information where required by law or where we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Munchappy, our users, or the public. See Section 14 for more detail.

### 5.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, sale of assets, or other business transaction involving Munchappy or The Candeed Corp, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify affected users of any such transfer and any material changes to this Privacy Policy that result from it.

## 7. Data Security

Munchappy takes the protection of your personal information seriously. We implement and maintain reasonable technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, loss, or destruction.

These measures include:

- **Encryption in transit** — all data transmitted between your device and Munchappy's systems is encrypted using industry-standard protocols;
- **Encryption at rest** — sensitive data stored in Munchappy's systems is encrypted at rest;
- **Access controls** — access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel and systems that require it to perform their function. Your personal data is accessible only to you and to Munchappy personnel with a legitimate operational need; and
- **Third-party security** — we require our third-party service providers to maintain appropriate security standards for any data they process on our behalf.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. While we work diligently to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, Munchappy will notify affected users in accordance with applicable law.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, to maintain your account, and to comply with our legal obligations.

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Customer account information | Retained while your account is active; deleted upon verified account deletion request |
| Customer order history | Minimum 3 years for tax, legal, and dispute resolution records |
| Courier account information | Retained while your account is active; deleted upon verified account deletion request |
| Courier delivery records | Minimum 3 years for legal, tax, and dispute resolution records |
| Courier identity verification results | Retained while the Courier account is active; upon account deletion, Munchappy requests removal of associated biometric identifiers from Didit's systems |
| Courier real-time location data | Not retained beyond the active delivery session; location data is used for dispatch and tracking purposes only and is not stored as a permanent record |
| Merchant account information | Retained while the merchant account is active; deleted upon verified account closure |
| Merchant transaction records | Minimum 3 years for tax, legal, and financial records |
| Support and dispute communications | Retained for a minimum of 3 years or as required by applicable law |

When your account is deleted or closed, Munchappy will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law or legitimate business necessity such as the resolution of an outstanding dispute or compliance with a legal obligation.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The Platform is intended for use by individuals who are at least **18 years of age**. Munchappy does not knowingly collect, solicit, or retain personal information from any person under the age of 18. If you are under 18, you may not use the Platform or create an account.

If Munchappy becomes aware that it has collected personal information from a person under the age of 18 without verifiable parental consent, it will take prompt steps to delete that information from its systems. If you believe that a minor has provided personal information to Munchappy, please contact us at **support@munchappy.com** and we will investigate and act accordingly.

Regardless of your location, all users of the Platform have the following rights with respect to their personal information:

- **Right to access** — you may request a copy of the personal information Munchappy holds about you;
- **Right to correction** — you may request that inaccurate or incomplete personal information be corrected;

## 12. New Jersey Data Privacy Act

The New Jersey Data Privacy Act (NJDPA), effective January 15, 2025, provides residents of New Jersey with specific rights regarding their personal data. In addition to the rights described in Section 11, New Jersey residents have the right to:

- **Opt out of targeted advertising** — Munchappy does not engage in targeted advertising. No opt-out is required, but you may contact us to confirm this at any time;
- **Opt out of the sale of personal data** — Munchappy does not sell personal data. No opt-out is required;
- **Opt out of profiling** — Munchappy does not engage in profiling for purposes of making decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects. No opt-out is required; and
- **Appeal a privacy rights decision** — if Munchappy declines to act on a privacy rights request, you may submit an appeal by contacting **support@munchappy.com** with the subject line "NJDPA Appeal." Munchappy will respond to your appeal within 45 days. If your appeal is denied, you may contact the New Jersey Division of Consumer Affairs for further assistance.

## 13. California Privacy Rights

If you are a resident of California, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provide you with the following additional rights:

- **Right to know** — you have the right to request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information Munchappy has collected about you, the sources of that information, the business purposes for which it is used, and the categories of third parties with whom it is shared;
- **Right to delete** — you have the right to request deletion of personal information Munchappy has collected from you, subject to certain exceptions;
- **Right to correct** — you have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal information;
- **Right to opt out of sale or sharing** — Munchappy does not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. No opt-out action is required;
- **Right to limit use of sensitive personal information** — Munchappy uses sensitive personal information, including precise geolocation data and identity verification data, only as necessary to provide the Platform's core services. You may contact us to learn more; and
- **Right to non-discrimination** — Munchappy will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA/CPRA rights.

To submit a CCPA/CPRA request, contact us at **support@munchappy.com** with the subject line "California Privacy Request." We will respond within 45 days. You may designate an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf, provided that agent can verify their authorization.

## 14. Law Enforcement and Legal Requests

Munchappy may disclose your personal information to law enforcement authorities, government agencies, or other third parties where:

- We are required to do so by applicable law, regulation, court order, subpoena, or other legal process;
- We believe disclosure is necessary to prevent or address imminent harm, fraud, or illegal activity;
- Disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Munchappy, our users, or the public; or
- The nature or gravity of a reported incident involving a Courier or other platform participant warrants cooperation with investigating authorities, including but not limited to incidents involving physical altercation, threats of violence, sexual misconduct, theft, or conduct that may constitute a criminal offense.

Where permitted by law, Munchappy will endeavor to notify affected users of a law enforcement request before disclosing their information. However, Munchappy may be legally prohibited from providing such notice in certain circumstances and reserves the right to comply with valid legal process without prior notice.
