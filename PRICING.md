# Munchappy Pricing

**Simple. Transparent. Fair.**

At Munchappy, we believe the people and businesses that earn through our platform should always know exactly what they pay to do so — no tiers, no hidden fees, no surprises. Every fee we charge is designed to cover the cost of building and maintaining a platform that works reliably for restaurants, couriers, and customers alike.

If you earn through Munchappy, you contribute a small share to keep it running. That is the entirety of our model.

---

## For Restaurants

Munchappy gives independent restaurants in the Jersey City and New York City metro area access to a customer base, a delivery network, and a payment infrastructure — without the crushing commission rates that define most major delivery platforms.

Industry-standard platforms charge restaurants between 15% and 30% per order — often more when hidden fees are factored in. Munchappy does not.

### What You Pay

> **7% of the order subtotal + $0.30 per order received through the platform.**

That is it. No setup fees. No monthly subscription. No marketing fees. No tiered plans. No penalty rates.

The 7% is calculated on the **menu subtotal only** — the price of the food items your customer ordered, before tax. Sales tax collected on your behalf is forwarded to you in full and is never subject to the Munchappy fee.

### What You Keep

The remainder — your menu prices, plus all sales tax collected — is transferred to you via Stripe Connect following each completed order, on a weekly basis in accordance with your payout schedule.

### What You Get

In exchange for the platform fee, Munchappy provides:

- **Customer access** — visibility to customers ordering delivery in your area through the Munchappy app
- **Order transmission** — reliable, real-time delivery of customer orders directly to your kitchen
- **Payment processing** — secure end-to-end payment collection and disbursement via Stripe, with no separate payment processing fee charged to you
- **Delivery coordination** — dispatch of independent couriers to handle every delivery, so you never need to manage a driver fleet
- **Platform infrastructure** — the technology, support, and operations that keep everything running

### How It Compares

| | Munchappy | Major Platforms |
|---|---|---|
| Commission per order | 7% of subtotal + $0.30 | 15% – 30%+ of order total |
| Setup fee | None | Varies |
| Monthly fee | None | Varies |
| Tax included in commission | No — tax forwarded in full | Often yes |
| Hidden fees | None | Often yes |

## For Couriers

Munchappy Courier connects independent delivery contractors with customers in the Jersey City and New York City metro area. The platform dispatches orders to you, collects the delivery fee from the customer on your behalf, and remits your earnings to you — minus a small platform fee for the infrastructure that makes it all possible.

### What You Pay

> **6% of the gross delivery fee + $0.30 per completed delivery.**

This fee is deducted automatically from the delivery fee collected from the customer before your earnings are remitted to you. You never write a check, send an invoice, or make a payment. Your net earnings are simply the gross delivery fee minus the platform fee — calculated and settled automatically for every delivery you complete.

### What You Are Guaranteed

Regardless of the platform fee, **your net earnings will never be less than $12.00 for any completed delivery.**

This is a contractual guarantee — not a promotional floor. Munchappy sets the gross delivery fee charged to the customer at a level that ensures the $12.00 net minimum is always met after the platform fee is applied. You do not subsidize this guarantee, and Munchappy does not absorb it. The fee is priced to make it work, every time.

For longer deliveries, your net earnings increase above the $12.00 minimum in accordance with Munchappy's distance-based rate structure. Your full net earnings for each delivery are displayed before you accept it.

### How Your Pay Works

Your earnings come entirely from the customer. When a customer places a delivery order, they pay a delivery fee as part of their order. Munchappy collects that fee on your behalf, deducts the platform fee upon completion of the delivery, and remits the remainder to you.

Munchappy does not contribute to your pay from its own funds. Every dollar you earn was paid by the customer who contracted for your service. The platform fee is what you contribute to access the customers, the dispatch technology, the payment infrastructure, and the operational support that makes the service possible.

### When You Get Paid

- **Weekly payout** — your earnings for each completed week are deposited via direct deposit on a weekly basis
- **Instant cashout** — available at any time for a small fee, disclosed before each transaction, allowing you to access your available earnings immediately

### What You Get

In exchange for the platform fee, Munchappy provides:

- **Dispatch access** — real-time delivery opportunities sent directly to you based on your location and availability
- **Guaranteed minimum** — a contractual $12.00 net earnings floor on every completed delivery
- **Full payout transparency** — your exact net earnings for each delivery shown before you accept
- **Payment collection** — Munchappy handles all billing, collection, and disbursement so you never have to chase payment
- **Reliable infrastructure** — the technology and operations that connect you to customers and restaurants

### No Tips. By Design.

Munchappy does not facilitate tipping. The delivery fee the customer pays already reflects the full value of your service — your earnings are built into the price of the order, not left to chance at the end of a delivery. What you see before you accept is what you earn when you complete.

## Why We Price This Way

Most delivery platforms are built to maximize what they extract from restaurants and couriers. The result is commission rates that make it difficult for independent restaurants to remain profitable, and earnings structures for couriers that are unpredictable at best.

Munchappy is built differently. Our fee exists for one reason: to cover the cost of operating a platform that reliably serves everyone on it. When restaurants succeed, they bring more orders to the platform. When couriers earn predictably, they deliver reliably. When customers have a good experience, they come back. That is the entire logic behind our pricing.

We are not trying to extract as much as possible from the people who make this platform work. We are trying to build something that works for everyone — and that means pricing it in a way that is honest about what we need and fair about what we take.
