# Munchappy Refund Policy

**Last Updated: April 25, 2026**
**Effective Date: April 25, 2026**
**Version: 1.0**

---

## Overview

This Refund Policy governs all refund and cancellation requests made in connection with orders placed through the Munchappy platform. It applies to both customers who place orders and restaurants that fulfill them. Please read it carefully, as it outlines your rights and obligations depending on your role on the platform.

Munchappy acts as a marketplace facilitator. Food and beverage items are sold directly by the restaurant, not by Munchappy. This distinction matters when determining who is responsible for a refund and how it is funded.

All refund requests must be submitted to **support@munchappy.com** within **24 hours** of the delivery or estimated delivery time, unless otherwise specified in this policy.

---

## Part I — Customer Refund Policy

### 1. Order Cancellations

#### 1.1 Cancellation Before Preparation Begins

You may request to cancel your order at any time before the restaurant marks it as "Preparing" in the Munchappy app. If your cancellation request is received and processed before that status update, your order will be cancelled and you will receive a refund of the order subtotal and applicable taxes, less a **$5.00 cancellation fee**.

The $5.00 cancellation fee is retained by Munchappy as an administrative fee to cover the cost of processing the order and initiating the cancellation. It is non-negotiable and non-refundable.

**What you receive:** Full order subtotal + taxes, minus $5.00.

#### 1.2 Cancellation After Preparation Has Begun

Once the restaurant has marked your order as "Preparing," cancellation is no longer guaranteed. At that point, the restaurant has already committed resources to fulfilling your order, and the decision to approve or deny a refund rests with the restaurant.

If you request a cancellation after preparation has begun:

- Munchappy will submit your request to the restaurant for review;
- The restaurant has **48 hours** to approve or deny the request;
- If the restaurant **approves** the refund, you will receive a full refund of the order subtotal and applicable taxes. The $5.00 cancellation fee still applies;
- If the restaurant **denies** the refund, you may escalate to Munchappy within **24 hours** of receiving the denial. See Section 1.3 for escalation details;
- If the restaurant does not respond within 48 hours, the request is treated as denied and you may proceed to escalation.

**What you receive if approved:** Full order subtotal + taxes, minus $5.00.
**What you receive if denied:** See Section 4 regarding delivery fee refunds.

#### 1.3 Escalation to Munchappy

If your refund request is denied by the restaurant and you believe the denial is unwarranted, you may escalate to Munchappy by contacting **support@munchappy.com** within **24 hours** of the denial. Your escalation request must include:

- Your order number;
- A clear description of the issue; and
- Any supporting evidence, including photographs where applicable.

Munchappy will review all submitted evidence and issue a final binding determination. If Munchappy determines in your favor, the cost of the refund is borne by the restaurant, not by Munchappy. Munchappy's determination is final.

---

### 2. Missing or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives with missing items or items that are materially different from what you ordered, you may be eligible for a partial refund covering the value of the affected items.

Missing and incorrect item refunds are handled directly by Munchappy and do not require restaurant approval. To request a partial refund:

- Contact **support@munchappy.com** within **24 hours** of delivery;
- Provide your order number and a description of the missing or incorrect items; and
- Include photographs where possible.

If your claim is verified, Munchappy will issue a partial refund for the value of the affected items. The cost of this refund is recovered from the restaurant through a deduction from their next scheduled payout. The restaurant will be notified of the deduction and the reason for it.

Refunds under this section cover the item value only. The delivery fee is not refunded where the remainder of the order was correctly delivered.

---

### 3. Undelivered Orders

If your order was never delivered and the courier cannot provide proof of delivery to your specified address, you are eligible for a full refund of the order subtotal, applicable taxes, and the delivery fee.

To request a refund for an undelivered order:

- Contact **support@munchappy.com** within **24 hours** of the estimated delivery time;
- Provide your order number and a description of the situation.

Munchappy will review delivery records and courier proof of delivery before processing any refund. If the failure is attributed to the courier, the courier forfeits their earnings for that delivery following manual review. Munchappy will process the refund upon completion of that review.

**What you receive:** Full order subtotal + taxes + delivery fee.

---

### 4. Delivery Fee Refunds

The $9.99 delivery fee is ordinarily non-refundable, as it compensates the courier for the service they performed. However, Munchappy will refund the delivery fee in the following circumstances:

- Your order was never delivered (see Section 3);
- Your refund request for the food items was denied by the restaurant and the complaint relates to the restaurant's product — including but not limited to wrong order, missing items, or food quality issues — where the courier completed the delivery as dispatched; or
- Your order arrived more than 30 minutes after the restaurant marked it as ready for pickup, as described in the Customer Terms of Service.

The delivery fee refund is issued by Munchappy as a goodwill measure in recognition that the delivery failure or product failure was not caused by you. It does not constitute an admission of liability by Munchappy.

---

### 5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

- You simply no longer want the order after it has been placed;
- You are dissatisfied with the taste, presentation, or style of food that was correctly prepared and delivered as ordered;
- You provided an incorrect or inaccessible delivery address and the courier was unable to complete delivery as a result;
- The order was marked as delivered and proof of delivery exists for your specified address;
- Your request was submitted more than 24 hours after delivery or estimated delivery; or
- Munchappy determines, following review, that the request is part of a pattern of refund abuse (see Section 6).

---

### 6. Refund Abuse

Munchappy monitors refund activity across all accounts. If an account accumulates three or more refund requests within any rolling 30-day period, further refund requests from that account will be flagged for manual review before any refund is issued. Munchappy reserves the right to deny refund requests and suspend or terminate accounts where a clear pattern of abuse is identified.

Initiating a chargeback with your bank or card issuer without first contacting Munchappy support and allowing a reasonable opportunity for resolution is a violation of the Customer Terms of Service and may result in account suspension.

---

### 7. How Refunds Are Processed

All approved refunds are returned to the **original payment method** used at the time of the order. Refunds are not issued as platform credits unless explicitly agreed to in writing.

Please allow **5–7 business days** for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your bank or card issuer. Munchappy initiates all approved refunds promptly, but processing times are determined by your financial institution and are outside of Munchappy's control.

## Part II — Merchant Refund Policy

### 8. Your Role in the Refund Process

As the seller of all food and beverage items on the Munchappy platform, restaurants bear primary financial responsibility for refunds arising from errors, quality issues, or incorrect items on their part. Munchappy facilitates the refund process but does not absorb costs that originate from restaurant-side failures.

### 9. Refund Approval and Response Window

When a customer submits a cancellation or refund request that requires restaurant approval, Munchappy will notify you promptly via the merchant portal and the email address associated with your account. You have **48 hours** from the time of notification to approve or deny the request.

- If you **approve** the refund, Munchappy will process the customer refund and deduct the applicable payment processing fee from your next scheduled payout. See Section 10 for details.
- If you **deny** the refund, the customer may escalate to Munchappy for a final review. If Munchappy overturns your denial, the cost of the refund remains your responsibility.
- If you **do not respond within 48 hours**, the request is treated as denied and the customer may proceed to escalation. Munchappy's determination on escalation is binding.

### 10. Processing Fees on Approved Refunds

When a customer refund is approved — whether by you directly or by Munchappy on escalation — the original payment processing fee charged on the transaction is non-recoverable. This fee is deducted from your next scheduled payout along with any refunded item amounts.

You will receive an itemized notification for every deduction made from your payout, including the order number, the refund amount, and the reason for the deduction. Processing fees are not charged by Munchappy — they are the cost of the original payment transaction and are passed through at cost.

### 11. Missing and Incorrect Item Deductions

Where Munchappy determines that a missing or incorrect item refund is warranted following a customer complaint, the value of the refunded item will be deducted from your next scheduled payout. You will be notified of the deduction and the specific item or items it relates to before the deduction is applied.

These deductions are made at Munchappy's discretion following review of the customer's claim and available evidence. Munchappy will not issue missing item refunds without reasonable verification.

### 12. Pattern of Refund-Related Complaints

Munchappy monitors refund and complaint activity across all restaurant accounts. A consistent pattern of customer complaints resulting in upheld refunds — whether approved by the restaurant or overturned by Munchappy on escalation — may be reviewed under the terms of the Merchant Agreement and may result in account review, additional requirements, or termination of the merchant relationship at Munchappy's discretion.

### 13. Delivery Fee Refunds

Where Munchappy issues a delivery fee refund to a customer as a goodwill measure following a restaurant-side product failure (see Section 4), that cost is borne by Munchappy and is not charged back to the restaurant. The delivery fee refund reflects a failure in the customer experience attributable to the product, not to the restaurant's financial obligation to Munchappy.
